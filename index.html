DTC is the abbreviation of Diagnositic Trouble Code

When the ECU recognizes and identifies a problem a DTC for that fault is stored in its memory. These codes are intended to help you determine the root cause of the failure. The diagnostic codes that are required by law on all OBDII systems are standardized.
